Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platform game occuring in a horizontal geometric-shaped world. It was released on August 13th, 2013 at the modest price of 1.99USD for iOs and Android and at 3.99USD on steam on December 22, 2014. A lite version of the game is also available for free as well as some expansions of the game including exclusives levels and mechanics.

The player takes the form of different entities or vehicules with unique mechanics such as the cube, the ship, the orb ball or most recently the spider.

Avoid all the spikes and threatening objects by clicking to make the cube jump! But don't forget to get along with the music!

The game has got 21 main levels going from the easiest difficulty to the Extreme Demon difficulty which is the hardest! Then you may take a shot in some levels created by other players through the Editor Mode ! Will you be able to beat them all ?
